[name::replace-by-multiple]
[SpocWebEntityId::22299]
[TrizPrinciple::[[Triz-inexpensive_short-lived_instead_of_expensive_durable]]]

Replace an inexpensive object with a multiple of inexpensive objects, comprising certain qualities (such as service life, for instance).

## Examples: 

- #has_/examples :: Use disposable Paper Objects to avoid the Cost of cleaning and storing durable Objects. Plastic Cups in Motels, disposable Diapers, many kinds of medical Supplies.
