[SpocWebEntityId::22273]
[TrizPrinciple::[[Triz-Moving_to_a_new_dimension]]]

Use a multi-story arrangement of objects instead of a single-story arrangement.

## Examples: 

- #has_/examples :: Electronic Chips on both Sides of a printed Circuit Board
- #has_/examples :: Employees 'disappear' from the Customers in a Theme Park, descend into a Tunnel, and walk to their next Assignment, where they return to the Surface and magically reappear.
- #has_/examples :: Cassette with 6 CD's to increase Music Time and Variety
- #has_/examples :: Stack Microelectronic hybrid Circuits to improve Density.
