[TrizPrinciple::[[Triz-Replace_a_mechanical_system]]]

Use electric, magnetic and electromagnetic fields to interact with the object.

## Examples: 

- #has_/examples :: To mix 2 Powders, electrostatically charge one positive and the other negative. Either use Fields to direct them, or mix them mechanically and let their acquired Fields cause the grains of Powder to pair up.
- #has_/examples :: The Transrapid / Maglev Rail uses magnetic Forces to drive and control the Wagons without Friction.
