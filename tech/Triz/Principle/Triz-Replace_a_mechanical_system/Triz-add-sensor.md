[name::add-sensor]
[SpocWebEntityId::22300]
[TrizPrinciple::[[Triz-Replace_a_mechanical_system]]]

Replace a mechanical means with a sensory (optical, acoustic, taste or smell) means.

## Examples: 

- #has_/examples :: Replace a physical Fence to confine a Dog or Cat with an acoustic 'Fence' (signal audible to the Animal).
- #has_/examples :: Use a bad smelling Compound in natural Gas to alert users to Leakage, instead of a mechanical or electrical Sensor.
