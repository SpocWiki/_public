[SpocWebEntityId::22329]
[TrizPrinciple::[[Triz-Inert_environment]]]

Add neutral parts, or inert additives to an object.

## Examples: 

- #has_/examples :: Increase the Volume of powdered Detergent by adding inert Ingredients. This makes it easier to measure with conventional Tools. 
- #has_/examples :: To prevent Cotton from catching Fire in a Warehouse, it is treated with inert Gas while being transported to the Storage Area.
