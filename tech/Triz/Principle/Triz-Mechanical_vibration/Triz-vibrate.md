[name::vibrate]
[SpocWebEntityId::22276]
[TrizPrinciple::[[Triz-Mechanical_vibration]]]

Cause an object to oscillate or vibrate.


## Examples: 

- #has_/examples :: Use a statistical Engine to generate mechanical Energy from thermal Vibrations.
- #has_/examples :: Overcome static Friction at lower Energies by introducing a constant Level of Energy.
- #has_/examples :: Vibrate a casting mold while it is being filled to improve flow and structural properties.
- #has_/examples :: Electric Carving Knife with vibrating Blades and electric Saw to allow the User to concentrate on Direction.
