[name::removed-obsolete]
[SpocWebEntityId::22314]
[TrizPrinciple::[[Triz-Rejecting_and_regenerating_parts]]]

Make Portions of an Object that have fulfilled their Functions go away (discard by dissolving, evaporating, etc.) or modify these directly during operation.

## Examples: 

- #has_/examples :: Bullet Casings are ejected after the Gun fires.
- #has_/examples :: Sprinkle Water on Cornstarch-based Packaging and watch it reduce its Volume by more than 1000*!
- #has_/examples :: Use a dissolving Capsule for Medicine.
- #has_/examples :: use Water Ice or Carbon Dioxide (Dry Ice) to make a Template for a rammed Earth Structure, such as a temporary Dam. Fill with Earth, then, let the Ice melt or sublime to leave the final Structure.
- #has_/examples :: Rocket Boosters separate after serving their Function.
