[SpocWebEntityId::22306]
[TrizPrinciple::[[Triz-Flexible_film_or_thin_membrane]]]

Isolate the object from the external Environment using flexible shells and thin films.

## Examples: 

- #has_/examples :: Float a Film of bipolar Material (one end hydrophilic, one end hydrophobic) on a Reservoir to limit Evaporation.
- #has_/examples :: Use a thin organic or anorganic Coating to protect Surfaces and thus the whole Structure.
