[TrizPrinciple::[[Triz-Inversion]]]

Make movable parts (or the external environment) fixed, and fixed parts movable).

## Examples: 

- #has_/examples :: Moving sidewalk with standing People.
- #has_/examples :: Rotate the Part instead of the Tool.
- #has_/examples :: Treadmill for Walking or Running in Place
- #has_/examples :: Abrasively cleaning Parts by vibrating the Parts instead of the Abrasive.
