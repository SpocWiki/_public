[SpocWebEntityId::22262]
[TrizPrinciple::[[Triz-Inversion]]]

Invert the action(s) used to solve the problem (e.g. instead of cooling an object, heat it).

## Examples: 

- #has_/examples :: To loosen stuck Parts, cool the inner Part instead of heating the outer Part.
- #has_/examples :: Bring the Mountain to Mohammed, instead of bringing Mohammed to the Mountain.
