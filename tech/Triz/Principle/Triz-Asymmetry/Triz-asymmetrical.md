[name::asymmetrical]
[SpocWebEntityId::22247]
[TrizPrinciple::[[Triz-Asymmetry]]]

A. Change the shape of an object from symmetrical to asymmetrical.

## Examples: 

- #has_/examples :: Asymmetrical Mixing Vessels or asymmetrical Vanes in symmetrical Vessels improve Mixing (Cement Trucks, Cake Mixers, Blenders).
- #has_/examples :: Make one Side of a Tire stronger than the other to withstand Impact with the Curb. 
- #has_/examples :: While discharging wet Sand through a symmetrical Funnel, the Sand forms an Arch above the Opening, causing irregular Flow. A Funnel of asymmetrical Shape eliminates the Arching Effect. 
- #has_/examples :: Put a flat Spot on a cylindrical Shaft to attach a Knob securely.
