[name::optimal-conditions]
[SpocWebEntityId::22268]
[TrizPrinciple::[[Triz-Dynamicity]]]

Allow (or design) the characteristics of an object, external environment, or process to change to be optimal or to find an optimal operating condition.

## Examples: 

- #has_/examples :: Adjustable steering wheel (or Seat, or Back Support, or Mirror Position...) 
- #has_/examples :: Use conical Railway Wheels that generate a central Force to stabilize the Wagons.
