[name::different-materials]
[SpocWebEntityId::22322]
[TrizPrinciple::[[Triz-Thermal_expansion]]]

If thermal expansion is being used, use multiple materials with different coefficients of thermal expansion.

## Examples: 

- #has_/examples :: The basic Leaf Spring Thermostat: BiMetal(2 Metals with different Coefficients of Expansion are linked so that it bends one Way when warmer than nominal and the opposite Way when cooler.
