[name::use-optical-copies]
[SpocWebEntityId::22297]
[TrizPrinciple::[[Triz-Copying]]]

Replace an object, or process with optical copies.

## Examples: 

- #has_/examples :: Measure an Object by measuring the Photograph or its Shadow.
- #has_/examples :: Make Sonograms to evaluate the Health of a Fetus, instead of risking Damage by direct Testing.
- #has_/examples :: Do surveying from Space Photographs instead of on the Ground.
