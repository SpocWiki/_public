[name::use-IR-UV-copies]
[SpocWebEntityId::22298]
[TrizPrinciple::[[Triz-Copying]]]

If visible optical copies are already used, move to infrared or ultraviolet copies.

## Examples: 

- #has_/examples :: Make Images in Infrared to detect Heat Sources, such as Diseases in Crops, or Intruders in a security System.
