[name::weigt-lift]
[SpocWebEntityId::22254]
[TrizPrinciple::[[Triz-Counterweight]]]

To compensate for the weight of an object, merge it with other objects that provide lift.

## Examples: 

- #has_/examples :: Inject foaming Agent into a Bundle of Logs, to make it float better. 
- #has_/examples :: Use Helium Balloon to support Advertising Signs.
