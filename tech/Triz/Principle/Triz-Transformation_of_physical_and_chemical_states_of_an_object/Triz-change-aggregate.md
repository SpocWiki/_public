[name::change-aggregate]
[SpocWebEntityId::22316]
[TrizPrinciple::[[Triz-Transformation_of_physical_and_chemical_states_of_an_object]]]

Change an Object's aggregate State(e.g. to a Gas, Liquid, or Solid).

## Examples: 

- #has_/examples :: Freeze the liquid Centers of filled Candies, then dip in melted Chocolate, instead of handling the messy, gooey, hot Liquid.
- #has_/examples :: Transport Oxygen or Nitrogen or Petroleum Gas as a Liquid, instead of a Gas, to reduce Volume.
