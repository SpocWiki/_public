[name::use-fluid]
[SpocWebEntityId::22304]
[TrizPrinciple::[[Triz-Use_pneumatic_or_hydralic]]]

Use gas and liquid parts of an object instead of solid parts (e.g. inflatable, filled with liquids, air cushion, hydrostatic, hydro-reactive).

## Examples: 

- #has_/examples :: To increase the Draft of an industrial Chimney, a Spiral Pipe with Nozzles was installed. When Air flows through the Nozzles, it creates an air-like Wall, reducing Drag.
- #has_/examples :: For shipping fragile Products, Air Bubble Envelopes or Foam-like Materials are used. Liquids and Gases are not worn and distribute directed Energy in all Directions.
- #has_/examples :: Comfortable Shoe Sole inserts filled with Gel
- #has_/examples :: Store Energy from decelerating a Vehicle in a hydraulic System, then use the stored Energy to accelerate later.
