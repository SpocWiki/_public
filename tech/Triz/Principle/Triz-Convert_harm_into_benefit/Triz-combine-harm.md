[name::combine-harm]
[SpocWebEntityId::22288]
[TrizPrinciple::[[Triz-Convert_harm_into_benefit]]]

Eliminate the primary harmful action by adding it to another harmful action to resolve the problem.

## Examples: 

- #has_/examples :: Add a buffering Material to a corrosive Solution.
- #has_/examples :: Use a Helium-Oxygen Mix for Diving, to eliminate both Nitrogen Narcosis and Oxygen Poisoning from Air and other Nitrox Mixes.
