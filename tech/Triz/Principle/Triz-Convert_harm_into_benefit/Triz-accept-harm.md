[name::accept-harm]
[SpocWebEntityId::22287]
[TrizPrinciple::[[Triz-Convert_harm_into_benefit]]]

Use harmful factors (particularly, harmful effects of the environment or surroundings) to achieve a positive effect.


## Examples: 

- #has_/examples :: Use Waste Heat to generate Electric Power.
- #has_/examples :: Recycle Waste (Scrap) Material from one Process as Raw Materials for another.
- #has_/examples :: Use high frequency Current Skin Effect to treat Metal Surfaces.
- #has_/examples :: Use turbulent Currents to heat Copper Pans and Pots or to brake electrical Trains.
- #has_/examples ::
