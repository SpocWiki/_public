[name::fractal-surface]
[SpocWebEntityId::22308]
[TrizPrinciple::[[Triz-Use_porous_material]]]

If an object is already porous, use the pores to introduce a useful substance or function. Fractal Structures like Pores have an infinite reactive Surface! 

## Examples: 

- #has_/examples :: To avoid pumping Coolant to a Machine, some of its Parts are filled with a porous Material soaked in coolant Liquid. The Coolant evaporates when the Machine is working, providing short-term uniform Cooling.
- #has_/examples :: Store Hydrogen in the Pores of a Palladium Sponge. (Fuel 'Tank' for the Hydrogen Car--much safer than storing Hydrogen Gas)
- #has_/examples :: Use a porous Metal Mesh to wick Excess Solder away from a Joint.
