[SpocWebEntityId::22291]
[TrizPrinciple::[[Triz-Feedback]]]

If feedback is already used, change its magnitude or influence.

## Examples: 

- #has_/examples :: Change a Management Measure from Budget Variance to Customer Satisfaction. 
- #has_/examples :: Change Sensitivity of an Autopilot when within 5 Miles of an Airport.
- #has_/examples :: Change Sensitivity of a Thermostat when cooling vs. heating, since it uses Energy less efficiently when cooling.
- #has_/examples :: Ice and Water are measured separately but must combine to the total Weight. Because Ice is difficult to dispense precisely, it is measured first. The Weight is then fed to the Water Control Device, which precisely dispenses the needed Amount.
