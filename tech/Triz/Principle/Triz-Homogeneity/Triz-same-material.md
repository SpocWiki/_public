[name::same-material]
[SpocWebEntityId::22313]
[TrizPrinciple::[[Triz-Homogeneity]]]

Make Objects interacting with a given object of the same material (or material with identical properties).

## Examples: 

- #has_/examples :: Make a Diamond Cutting Tool out of Diamonds.
- #has_/examples :: Make the Container out of the same Material as the Contents, to reduce chemical Reactions.
- #has_/examples :: The Surface of a Feeder for abrasive Grain is made of the same Material that runs through the Feeder, allowing a continuous Restoration of the Surface.
