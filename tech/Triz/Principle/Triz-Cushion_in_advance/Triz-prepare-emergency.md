[name::prepare-emergency]
[SpocWebEntityId::22260]
[TrizPrinciple::[[Triz-Cushion_in_advance]]]

Prepare emergency means beforehand to compensate for the relatively low reliability of an object.

## Examples: 

- #has_/examples :: Alternate Air System for Aircraft Instruments 
- #has_/examples :: Back-up Parachute
- #has_/examples :: Magnetized Merchandise to prevent Shop Lifting
- #has_/examples :: Magnetized Tools to hold Screws
- #has_/examples :: Magnetic Strip on photographic Film that directs the Developer to compensate for poor Exposure
