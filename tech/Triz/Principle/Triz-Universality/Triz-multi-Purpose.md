[name::multi-urpose]
[SpocWebEntityId::22251]
[TrizPrinciple::[[Triz-Universality]]]

Make a part or object perform multiple functions; eliminate the need for other parts.

## Examples: 

- #has_/examples :: CCD (Charge coupled device) with micro-lenses formed on the surface 
- #has_/examples :: Convertible Bed-Sofa or Car 
- #has_/examples :: Child's car safety seat converts to a stroller
- #has_/examples :: Mulching lawnmower
- #has_/examples :: Minivan Seat for seating, sleeping, carrying Cargo
- #has_/examples :: Team leader acts as recorder and timekeeper.
- #has_/examples :: Handle of a toothbrush contains toothpaste
- #has_/examples ::
