[name::dip into Paint]
[SpocWebEntityId::22438]
[Triz-Principle-Sub::[[Triz-accept-less]]]

A Cylinder is painted by dipping into Paint, but contains more Paint than desired. Excess Paint is then removed by rapidly rotating the Cylinder.
